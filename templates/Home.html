IN - HOUSE TRENDING TECHNOLOGIES

We offer a wide range of both modern and antique design technologies to our customer base with dynamicness depending on the customers perspectives and needs .

Open-Plan Living

Combining living, dining, and kitchen areas into one large, multi-functional space. This creates a sense of spaciousness and promotes social interaction.

Bioliphic Living

Blurring the lines between indoor and outdoor spaces with features like large windows, sliding doors, patios, and balconies. This allows residents to enjoy the Kenyan climate and landscape.

Roof Top Terraces & Gardens

Utilizing rooftop spaces for recreation, gardening, and entertainment. This maximizes space and provides stunning views.

Modern Minimalist Design

Clean lines, simple forms, open spaces, and a focus on functionality. Often incorporates natural light and ventilation. Doesnt requires large spaces and is suitable for small families or semi permanent living

Geometric Patterns and Designs

Incorporating geometric shapes and patterns into facades, interiors, and landscaping. This adds visual interest and creates a modern aesthetic.

Green Building and Sustainability

Designs that prioritize energy efficiency, water conservation, and waste reduction. Features like solar panels, rainwater harvesting, and green roofs are becoming increasingly popular.
